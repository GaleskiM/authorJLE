What Happens In The Woods

Five friends go on a vacation and get lost in the woods. They make a new friend along the way who has secrets of their own. They might fight to protect themselves and their new friend.

Walls Bleed Red

New England native George Dean lives a normal life as an insurance salesman in Bethel, Maine. His wife Paula and their two sons - Frankie and Ben - complete the picture, leaving him with the role of content family man. When George's expertise is requested by that of an over-eager Mr. Hansen, he has no idea his life is about to change in Castine, Maine over what was supposed to be a simple insurance quote.

As George and his family turn the business travel into an opportunity for relaxation together, they soon realize their expected vacation will be far from fun. George soon discovers his naivete in multi-tasking a family trip on top of business, despite some summer season still remaining.

Unbeknownst to the happy family, the house originally coined a "getaway" has secrets all its own, and it’s dying to let them out.

In Her Head

Jade's twin brother Jackson went missing on a bus trip to New York 6 months ago. With the police not accomplishing anything, Jade has decided to look for her brother herself. Will she find him? Or will she end up with the same fate?

They Came For Her

It’s been almost two years and Jade is finally coming to terms with the past and the death of her twin brother. With a loving boyfriend and a baby, Jade is trying to focus on the future and all the good things happening around her. Just when she thinks everything is going right she starts receiving mysterious letters again. Jade doesn’t like the cruel joke someone is trying to play on her. Is it a joke or is someone out to finish what Detective Lather’s started?
